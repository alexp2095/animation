Zombie

Movement for Classical Renewal Manifesto 2002

In order for the zombie animation to work, I had to find a zombie image that had all the frames combined into one photo. Once I found one, I had to set the width and height in order for the website to show only one frame, instead of the entire sprite sheet. Then I added the margins, to center it properly within the page. I then added the url of the zombie to the background and adjusted it to left center.

After, I added the keyframe named play that would animate the background until it reached the end (-2000px). Then I bound the play animation sequence to the zombie selector. I then added the .8s, which means the duration of the animation. Then I added steps(10) in order to change the scrolling effect into an actual animation that went through each frame. After, I placed infinite in order to make the animation repeat continuously.

The reason as to why I chose a zombie as an animation to represent the manifesto was because it claimed that the classical art was slowly dying and being replaced by conceptualism and modernism. Because of this, I wanted to represent the dying movement as a zombie.
